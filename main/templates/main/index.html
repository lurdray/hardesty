About Me

Odewoye Adebola OluwaToyosi better known as Hardestee is a Physicist, Instructor, Entertainer and Philanthropist. He Obtained Bachelor in Physics/Electronics from Federal University of Technology,Minna in 2018. He also attended Driving School Instructor training with FRSC in 2017. He work with Dartford Mortgage Centre in United Kingdom, Funmi Nehan Foundation etc..He's the Founder/Proprietor of Hardestee Samuel International (Hardestee Driving School,Hardestee Charity, Hardestee Farm, Hardestee Comedy) and Senior Instructor at Jupiter Safety Driving School. He has talent in Comedy and Music with over 20 release inspirational songs and video. World is War(Ogun Laye) was his first official Musical video
